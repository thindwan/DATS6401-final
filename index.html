Why Olympics

Olympic Games are a leading international sporting event featuring summer and winter sports competitions in which thousands of athletes both men and women around the world participate. Since 1896 more than 35,000 medals have been awarded at the Olympics. It is important to understand the relationship between the factors contributing to the performance of athletes in the competition based on GDP, education, healthcare, and Season.

Primary objective of this project is to visualize the countries who have won the medals and factors contributing to their performances which will give a prediction for future Olympic games The variables analyzed will be the type of Olympics, GDP per Capita, gender, healthcare, location, and education. Understanding the relationship between these factors may help to predict the chances of a country winning a medal in the future Olympic games .Following is the analysis of data through Tabelue
